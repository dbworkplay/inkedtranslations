What I say about myself

Logo Designer / Brand Builder / Ex developer / Self taught

I’m a self-taught, independent graphic designer, passionate about brands and building their visual identities. I enjoy working as a freelancer, helping my clients to achieve their goals and to elevate their brands on the next level.

Before design career I worked as a back-end Java developer, programming web applications and web services. As a designer I still use logical thinking, and I love to use geometry, golden ratio circles and proportions. I love to sketch all of my ideas on the paper and I create all of my work using the grids for perfect visual balance.
